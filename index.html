Welcome to our summer camp "Viking Weekend" for children between 6 and 11 years old!

Here we let the children live out their Viking fantasies while at the same time learning what made the Vikings special, everything from sailing, building their own shields to camaraderie.
As a parent/guardian, it is important that your child/children bring "weather-appropriate clothing" as well as a sleeping bag and sanitary products such as a toothbrush and toothpaste, then you as a parent/guardian can feel secure that our trained child educators will take care of your child/ children.
From ten years of experience, we can guarantee that "Viking Weekend" creates memories, knowledge and friendships that will last a lifetime.
