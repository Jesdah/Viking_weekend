The adventure begins here!

The day begins with our adventure leaders introducing themselves to the children, then through play the children get to know each other. After that, it's time to get dressed! Put on your finest armor and your scariest face paint and we'll jump into a real Viking ship and sail away. In the evening we gather around the camp fire and listen to Harald Vikingsson's stories.

The adventure is not over!

The day starts with a proper breakfast that satisfies most warriors, then it's time for archery, ax throwing and fencing. You're not a real Viking if you don't have a shield, so use paint, imagination and build your own shield. In the evening there is a party in the Longhouse and everyone is invited! There will be music and mead (juice and soda, of course).
